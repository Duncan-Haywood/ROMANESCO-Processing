*INFO

*P > Prescene
*S > Scene
When you're not in live mode Prescene is the Scene




*CAMERA *P

'c' + click left 
>translate main camera

'c' + click right
>rotate main camera

'c' + '0' 
>back to the preset settting camera

'b'
>swich rotateY and rotate2 of main camera

'SPACE'
>stop and reset inertia

'v' + click left 
>translate item camera

'v' + click right
>rotate item camera

'v' + '0' 
>back to the preset settting camera
